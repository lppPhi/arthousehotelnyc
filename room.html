Modern New York Hotel Rooms

Relax in Boutique Luxury on Manhattan's Upper West Side

Arthouse Hotel New York City sets a new standard for style and comfort among boutique hotels on Manhattan’s Upper West Side. Modeled after the famously space-savvy designs of New York City apartments, our hotel rooms, lofts and suites marry relaxing décor with modern luxuries. Some feature private outdoor balconies with seating, while others afford breathtaking views of Central Park, the Hudson River or Upper West Side skyline.

All rooms at Arthouse Hotel New York City feature wood floors and modern furnishings and artwork. Sophisticated design touches include contemporary light wood furniture, crisp white linens and opulent blue drapes. Expansive, industrial-style windows fill living spaces with plenty of natural light. Amenities include 42" flat-screen HD televisions, Keurig coffee makers, Wi-Fi, Pure bath products and bottled water.
